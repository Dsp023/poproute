## üìÑ Must-Read Papers

### Foundational

1. **"Attention Is All You Need" (2017)** - Vaswani et al.
   - Introduced Transformers
   - Revolutionary architecture
   - Deep dive in: [Transformers](../Transformers/01-beginner-transformer-basics.md)

2. **"ImageNet Classification with Deep CNNs" (AlexNet, 2012)** - Krizhevsky et al.
   - Deep learning breakthrough
   - Started modern AI era
   - Covered in: [Computer Vision](../Computer-Vision/02-intermediate-cv-techniques.md)

3. **"Deep Residual Learning for Image Recognition" (ResNet, 2015)** - He et al.
   - Skip connections
   - Enabled very deep networks
   - Explained in: [Computer Vision](../Computer-Vision/02-intermediate-cv-techniques.md)

### Language Models

4. **"BERT: Pre-training of Deep Bidirectional Transformers" (2018)** - Devlin et al.
   - Bidirectional language understanding
   - Transfer learning for NLP
   - Detailed in: [Transformers](../Transformers/) and [LLMs](../Large-Language-Models/)

5. **"Language Models are Few-Shot Learners" (GPT-3, 2020)** - Brown et al.
   - Demonstrated scaling laws
   - Few-shot learning capabilities
   - Covered in: [LLMs](../Large-Language-Models/01-beginner-llm-basics.md)

6. **"Training Language Models to Follow Instructions with Human Feedback" (InstructGPT, 2022)** - Ouyang et al.
   - RLHF methodology
   - Foundation for ChatGPT
   - Detailed in: [LLMs](../Large-Language-Models/03-advanced-llm-fine-tuning.md)

### RAG and Retrieval

7. **"Retrieval-Augmented Generation for Knowledge-Intensive NLP Tasks" (2020)** - Lewis et al.
   - Introduced RAG
   - Combining retrieval and generation
   - Comprehensive guide: [RAG Systems](../RAG-Systems/)

### Computer Vision

8. **"Very Deep Convolutional Networks for Large-Scale Image Recognition" (VGG, 2014)** - Simonyan & Zisserman
   - Deep uniform architecture
   - Still widely used
   - In: [Computer Vision](../Computer-Vision/02-intermediate-cv-techniques.md)

9. **"An Image is Worth 16x16 Words: Transformers for Image Recognition at Scale" (ViT, 2020)** - Dosovitskiy et al.
   - Transformers for vision
   - Alternative to CNNs
   - Covered: [Computer Vision](../Computer-Vision/03-advanced-cv-applications.md)

### Generative Models

10. **"Generative Adversarial Networks" (2014)** - Goodfellow et al.
    - Introduced GANs
    - Revolutionized generative modeling
    - In: [Deep Learning](../Machine-Learning/03-advanced-ml-deep-learning.md)

11. **"Denoising Diffusion Probabilistic Models" (2020)** - Ho et al.
    - Diffusion models
    - Used in DALL-E 2, Stable Diffusion
    - Covered: [Computer Vision](../Computer-Vision/03-advanced-cv-applications.md)
